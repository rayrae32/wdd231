Harmonic Soundscapes: Personal Music Portfolio

A showcase of original music tracks, videos, and performance dates.

Site Name

Harmonic Soundscapes

This name encapsulates the diverse range of musical genres and instruments featured in the portfolio, creating a strong identity that is creative and memorable for visitors. It highlights both harmony and the artistic landscapes of sound.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide an online portfolio of original music tracks organized by genre (classical, electronic, acoustic), allowing users to explore the music with added features like play buttons and details through modal dialogs. The site will also host embedded music videos, artist biographies, a contact form for event bookings, and an API-powered schedule of upcoming performances and releases.

Scenarios

These are some possible questions visitors might have:

  • How can I listen to the artist's latest electronic and acoustic tracks?
  • When and where is the next live performance?
  • How can I contact the artist for collaborations or event bookings?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #1a8cff (used for headings, buttons, and key accents)

Secondary Color: #e6f7ff (used for background highlights and call-to-action areas)

The selected colors reflect a clean, modern look with a vibrant primary blue for emphasis and a soft secondary blue to maintain clarity and accessibility.

Typography

The site will use the following fonts:

  • Headings: Arial, sans-serif – a clean and modern font used to emphasize sections and grab attention.
  • Body Text: Georgia, serif – a traditional font with a refined style, used for paragraphs and longer content to enhance readability.
